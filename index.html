German cars are known the world over as premium luxury vehicles. The German automotive industry has gained a reputation for comfort, efficiency, style, safety, and status.

The German automobile industry continues to lead the world in auto making. From innovative designs to sleek styling, Germany is the country to beat. As the years roll by, Germany only solidifies its well deserved reputation.

Audi is a German automobile manufacturer that designs, engineers, produces, markets and distributes luxury vehicles. Audi is a subsidiary of Volkswagen Group and has its roots at Ingolstadt, Bavaria, Germany. Audi vehicles are produced in nine production facilities worldwide.

BMW is a German multinational corporation which produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles. BMW is headquartered in Munich and produces motor vehicles in Germany, Brazil, China, India, Mexico, the Netherlands, South Africa, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

Volkswagen is a German motor vehicle manufacturer founded in 1937 by the German Labour Front, known for the iconic Beetle and headquartered in Wolfsburg. It is the flagship brand of the Volkswagen Group, the largest car maker by worldwide sales in 2016 and 2017.

Porsche is a German automobile manufacturer specializing in high-performance sports cars, SUVs and sedans. The headquarters of Porsche AG is in Stuttgart. Porsche's current lineup includes the 718 Boxster/Cayman, 911, Panamera, Macan, Cayenne and Taycan.
